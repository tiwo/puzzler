Changes Since Release 1
=======================

Added puzzles:

* Polysticks of order 1 through 4 in a 6 |x| 6 grid
  (bin/polysticks6x6.py).

* Tetrahexes (4 |x| 7 & clipped 3 |x| 10) and pentahexes (10 |x| 11 &
  5 |x| 22).

Began adding unit tests.


Release 1 (2006-08-08)
======================

The first release of Polyform Puzzler.  For the pre-history, see
section 1.2 of the `FAQ <FAQ.html>`_.
