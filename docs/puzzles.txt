.. contents::

Polyform Puzzler solves a variety of polyomino, polyiamond, polyhex,
polycube, and polystick puzzles.  Files containing all solutions of
most puzzles are available at
http://puzzler.sourceforge.net/solutions.

-------------

Pentominoes
===========

* Rectangles:

  - 3x20: 2 solutions

    .. image:: images/pentominoes-3x20.png

  - 4x15: 368 solutions

    .. image:: images/pentominoes-4x15.png

    .. image:: images/pentominoes-5x12.png

Solid Pentominoes
=================

* Solids:

  - 2x3x10: 10 solutions

    .. image:: images/solid-pentominoes-2x3x10.png

  - 2x5x6: 264 solutions

    .. image:: images/solid-pentominoes-2x5x6.png

  - 3x4x5: 3940 solutions

    .. image:: images/solid-pentominoes-3x4x5.png

* Ring walls (rectangular walls, 1 cube thick, with hollow centers):

  - 3x3x9: 3 solutions

    .. image:: images/solid-pentominoes-3x3x9-ring.png

  - 3x5x7: 1 solution

    .. image:: images/solid-pentominoes-3x5x7-ring.png

  - 5x3x5: 186 solutions

    .. image:: images/solid-pentominoes-5x3x5-ring.png

  - 6x3x4: 46 solutions

    .. image:: images/solid-pentominoes-6x3x4-ring.png

  Three other rings with no solutions were also implemented: 3x4x8,
  3x6x6, and 5x4x4.

Hexiamonds
==========

* 4x9 parallelogram: 74 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-4x9.png

* 6x6 parallelogram: 156 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-6x6.png

* 4x11 trapezium: 76 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-4x11-trapezium.png

* 4x10 elongated hexagon (clipped parallelogram): 856 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-4x10-longhex.png

* Snowflake (hexagon with tabs, radially symmetrical; a.k.a `Kadon's
  "Iamond Hex" <http://www.gamepuzzles.com/esspoly.htm#IH>`__): 55
  solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-snowflake.png

* Ring 2 (8-unit hexagon with 4-unit hexagonal hole offset one unit
  from the center): 11 solutions (no solutions for the ring with a
  centered hole)

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-ring-2.png

* Crescent (8-unit hexagon with a 4-unit hexagonal bite removed from
  one corner): 87 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-crescent.png

* Crescent 2 (8-unit hexagon with a 4-unit hexagonal bite removed from
  one side): 2 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-crescent-2.png

* 6x8 "coin" (elongated hexagon with central hexagonal hole):
  304 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-coin.png

* Trefoil (3 joined hexagons): 640 solutions

  .. image:: images/hexiamonds-trefoil.png

------------
 Polysticks
------------

Tetrasticks
===========

* 4x4 square with one-sided welded tetrasticks: 4 solutions

* 5x5 square with 15 of 16 tetrasticks: 1795 solutions


Polysticks of Order 1 Through 4
===============================

This puzzle uses the monostick and all distick, tristick, and
tetrastick pieces.

* 6x6 square covered with polysticks: many solutions, exact
  number unknown (I left it running for 3 days and it produced over
  35,000 solutions, without moving the first 3 pieces [X, I, I1]!).
