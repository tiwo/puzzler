Tetrahexes

  <http://puzzler.sf.net/solutions/tetrahex-4x7.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/tetrahex-4x7.png

* 3x10 parallelogram with corners clipped (centered rows of

  .. image:: images/tetrahex-3x10-clipped.png
