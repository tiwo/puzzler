**Polytrigs** are polysticks on a triangular grid, also known as
"triangular polysticks".  The name "trig" comes from "TRIangular
Grid"; a neologism I invented because "triangular polysticks" is too
unwieldy, especially when combined with order-prefixes (mono-, di-,
tri-, tetra-, etc.; "triangular tristicks"!?).  I hope the name
catches on.

    If the square polysticks dont get the respect they deserve, this
    is doubly true for the triangular polysticks.

    -- Alexandre Owen Mu√±iz, `Puzzle Zapper Blog
       <http://puzzlezapper.com/blog/2010/12/polysticks-on-a-regular-spanning-subgraph/>`__

I believe I am the first to address this type of puzzle so thoroughly
(if not, please let me know!).  Hopefully these puzzles will begin to
fill this particular gap in the polyform puzzle branch of recreational
mathematics.


Tritrigs
========

These puzzles use the 12 tritrigs, for a total of 36 line segments on
the triangular grid.

* 3x1 semi-regular hexagon ring: `1 solution
  <http://puzzler.sf.net/solutions/tritrigs-hex-3x1-ring.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/tritrigs-hex-3x1-ring.png

* 5x3 trapezoid ring: `1 solution
  <http://puzzler.sf.net/solutions/tritrigs-trapezoid-5x3-ring.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/tritrigs-trapezoid-5x3-ring.png

* Stacked 2x2x1 elongated hexagons: `9 solutions
  <http://puzzler.sf.net/solutions/tritrigs-stacked-elongated-hexagons-2x2x1.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/tritrigs-stacked-elongated-hexagons-2x2x1.png

* 5x3 jagged trapezoid: `1 solution

  .. image:: images/tritrigs-jagged-trapezoid-5x3.png

* Spiked triangle: `6 solutions
  <http://puzzler.sf.net/solutions/tritrigs-spiked-triangle-1.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/tritrigs-spiked-triangle-1.png


One-Sided Tritrigs
==================

7 of the 12 tritrigs are asymmetrical, therefore there are 19
one-sided tritrigs.

* 4x1 semi-regular hexagon: `solutions incomplete
  <http://puzzler.sf.net/solutions/one-sided-tritrigs-hex-4x1.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/one-sided-tritrigs-hex-4x1.png
