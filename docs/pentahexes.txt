* Two length-10 triangles add up to the right number of unit hexagons:
  `solutions incomplete <../solutions/pentahexes-two-triangles.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/pentahexes-two-triangles.png

  If the two triangles are pushed together, solutions to this puzzle
  are also solutions to the 10x11 parallelogram.

  This design by Christoph M. Hoffman appeared in Martin Gardner's
  "Mathematical Games" column, Scientific American, June 1967.
