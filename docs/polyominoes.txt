Polyominoes of Order 1 Through 4
================================

These puzzles use the 1 monomino, 1 domino, 2 trominoes, and 5
tetrominoes, for a total of 29 squares.

* Square-plus (design from Kadon / Kate Jones): `563 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-square-plus.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-square-plus.png

* 7x3-plus (design by Dan Klarskov): `17 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-7x3-plus.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-7x3-plus.png

* Skewered square (design by Dan Klarskov): `1,320 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-skewered-square.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-skewered-square.png

* Skewered 9x3 rectangle (design by Dan Klarskov): `5,249 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-skewered-9x3.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-skewered-9x3.png

* Skewered 7x3 rectangle: `747 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-skewered-7x3.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-skewered-7x3.png

* Astroid (design from Kadon / Kate Jones): `18 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-astroid.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-astroid.png

* 7x5 with cross-shaped hole (design by Dan Klarskov): `19 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-7x5-cross-hole.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-7x5-cross-hole.png

* 7x4 plus one (design by Dan Klarskov): `1,522 solutions
  <../solutions/polyominoes-1234-7x4-plus-one.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/polyominoes-1234-7x4-plus-one.png

  This is just a 7x4 rectangle using the polyominoes of order 2
  through 4, plus the monomino separately.  I thought it was cute.
